Padel 101
New to the
game?

Padel is doubles, played in a glass-walled enclosed court. Smaller than tennis, more social, faster to learn — and impossibly addictive.

🎾Played on a 10m × 20m enclosed court
👥Always doubles — 4 players per match
🧱Glass walls are in play — use them
🏸Solid rackets, no strings
⚡Easiest racket sport to pick up
🌍25M+ players worldwide
25M
Players Worldwide
Padel is now the world's fastest-growing sport, played in 90+ countries.
